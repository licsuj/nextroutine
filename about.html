Our story

Built for the person who keeps
meaning to start.

NextRoutine exists because most morning routine advice is either toxic (5am grind culture) or useless (another 47-step protocol). We wanted something different.

Why we exist

Screens took our mornings. We want them back.

We built NextRoutine after noticing a pattern: everyone we knew wanted a better morning routine, but almost nobody had one that lasted past day three. The reason was always the same — the phone won.

The insight that changed everything: screen time and morning routines aren't two separate problems. They're one problem. Your phone fills the void where your morning routine should be. Give your morning structure, and the phone habit breaks naturally.

That's the entire philosophy of NextRoutine. Not a blocker. Not a lecture. A structured replacement that makes the scrolling irrelevant.

What we believe

1

Consistency beats intensity

A 20-minute routine done every day is worth infinitely more than a 2-hour routine done twice a month.

2

Shame doesn't work

You're not weak for checking your phone. You're outmatched by billion-dollar engineering. Our approach has zero lectures.

3

Replace, don't restrict

Habits are replaced, not deleted. We give you something better to do — the old habit loses its grip on its own.

Honest answers.

What you'll get, who it's for, and whether the guy writing it is qualified.

What will I actually get?

One email per week, every Tuesday. Each one has a single protocol — something you can run tomorrow morning, that night, or this weekend. No 5,000-word essays. No book recommendations. Just the protocol.

Who is this for?

First-time fathers, expecting fathers, and dads in the first year. Specifically built for the kind of dad who'd subscribe to Naval, James Clear, or Huberman Lab — but for the fatherhood transition. If you're past the first year, you can still subscribe, but the early stages are where the protocols hit hardest.

Is the author qualified?

No. I'm not a doctor, a child psychologist, or a parenting expert. I'm a software engineer becoming a first-time dad, applying research to my own life in real time. If a protocol doesn't survive contact with the routine I'm running today, it doesn't ship. When the baby arrives, the bar gets higher. That's the whole credential.

How is my email used?

Stored in Beehiiv (newsletter platform, GDPR-compliant). I never sell, share, or rent it. Unsubscribe is one click and instant — link in every email. Full details in the Privacy Policy.

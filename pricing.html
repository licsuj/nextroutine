Questions, answered.

Honest answers about how this works.

Is the free newsletter really free forever?

Yes. The free weekly newsletter is free forever — no card, no upsell pressure. It's the foundation of NextRoutine and stays free even after Pro launches.

When does Pro launch?

When it's actually built. Realistically that's a few months out — I'm a one-person operation building this around my real life as an expectant father. Waitlist members get an email the moment Pro is ready, with founding-member access for the first 100.

What does "first 100 lifetime free" actually mean?

The first 100 people on the waitlist get full Pro access — every protocol, template, briefing — free. Forever. Not "12 months free" or "free until we change our minds." If you're in the first 100, you get the full Pro experience for as long as Pro exists.

Will I get charged anything before Pro launches?

No. There's no payment of any kind during the waitlist period. No credit card collection, no Stripe, no "save your spot for €1." Just an email address.

What if I'm not in the first 100?

You'll be notified when Pro launches and have the chance to subscribe at the regular €7.99/mo or €59.99/year price. Lifetime founding access is genuinely limited to the first 100 to keep the offer meaningful.

How do I know I'm in the first 100?

I'll email you to confirm your founding-member status when Pro is ready. Until then, joining the waitlist early is the surest way to be in.

Why are you doing this instead of charging from day one?

Because Pro doesn't exist yet, and selling something that doesn't exist isn't honest. I'd rather build a 100-person founding cohort who got in early and trust me, than rush a half-finished product to market.

Is my data safe?

Email addresses are stored in Beehiiv (newsletter platform, GDPR compliant). I never sell, share, or rent your information. Unsubscribe is one click and instant. NextRoutine operates from Europe and follows GDPR.

How it works

How do I get credit?

When someone signs up for the free newsletter through your unique link, you get credit. They need to confirm their email address for it to count.

When do I get my reward?

Rewards are applied automatically within 24 hours of hitting a milestone. You will receive an email confirming your Pro access.

Does "Pro free forever" really mean forever?

Yes. As long as NextRoutine exists, your Pro access stays active. No expiry, no renewal needed.

Can I refer someone who is already subscribed?

No — the referral link only counts new subscribers. If they are already on the list, it will not generate a credit.

What does the person I refer get?

They get the same thing every new subscriber gets: the weekly newsletter, the Fatherhood Starter Pack, 2 routine templates, and morning basics. They do not need to pay anything.

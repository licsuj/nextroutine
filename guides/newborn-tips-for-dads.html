Newborn Tips for Dads: Simple Routines for the First 3 Months

The newborn stage is not about doing everything perfectly. It is about creating a few repeatable supports when everyone is tired.

Create a night support plan

Know what you can own during nights: diapers, burping, bottle prep, water, snacks, logging, or settling after feeds.

Protect recovery time

Your partner may need rest, food, hydration, emotional reassurance, and fewer decisions. Make one recovery block easier each day.

Bond through repetition

Bonding does not need to be dramatic. A repeated walk, song, bath routine, or morning hold builds familiarity.

Reset the home daily

One small daily reset can change the mood: bottles, laundry, dishes, trash, meals, or restocking the changing station.

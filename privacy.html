1. Who We Are

NextRoutine is operated by NextRoutine Ltd, a company registered in Malta. We provide daily routine plans, habit systems, and email content for new and expectant fathers via nextroutine.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what we need to deliver the service. Here is every category of personal data we process:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to the free newsletter, sign up for Pro, or contact us.
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Stripe. We never see, receive, or store your card number, expiry date, or CVV. We receive only a confirmation of payment status and your Stripe customer ID.
  • Analytics data — page views, session duration, approximate location (country level), device type, and referral source via Google Analytics 4. Only collected after you accept analytics cookies.
  • Local storage data — cookie consent preference, email submission state, and quiz results stored in your browser. This data never leaves your device.

We do not collect your name, phone number, physical address, or any data beyond what is listed above.

3. How and Why We Use Your Data

Every use of your data has a lawful basis under GDPR:

  • To send the weekly newsletter and starter pack — lawful basis: your consent, given when you enter your email address and submit the form.
  • To manage your Pro subscription and process payments — lawful basis: performance of a contract (the subscription you purchased).
  • To understand how visitors use the site and improve content — lawful basis: legitimate interest, balanced against your rights. Analytics are only active after you explicitly accept cookies.
  • To respond to your emails — lawful basis: legitimate interest (you contacted us and expect a response).

We never sell your data. We never share your data with advertisers. We never use your email for purposes other than what you signed up for.

4. Email and Newsletter

We use a third-party email service provider (ConvertKit or Mailchimp) to send the NextRoutine newsletter and transactional emails. Your email address is stored on their servers under a Data Processing Agreement.

You can unsubscribe from any email using the unsubscribe link at the bottom of every message. Upon unsubscribing, your email is removed from our active mailing list within 48 hours and fully deleted from the provider within 30 days.

We do not send unsolicited emails. We do not share your email with other companies. We do not add your email to any list you did not explicitly subscribe to.

5. Payments (Stripe)

Pro and Pro+ subscriptions are processed by Stripe, Inc. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security certification.

When you enter your card details at checkout, that data goes directly to Stripe. NextRoutine never sees, processes, or stores your card number. We receive only:

  • A Stripe customer ID (a random string, not your card number)
  • Confirmation of payment status (succeeded, failed, refunded)
  • Subscription status (active, cancelled, trialling)

Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

6. Cookies and Analytics (GA4)

We use two categories of cookies:

Strictly necessary (always active): Cookie consent preference stored in your browser's localStorage. This is not a tracking cookie. It remembers whether you accepted or declined analytics so we do not ask you again on every visit.

Analytics (optional — off by default): Google Analytics 4. This is only loaded after you click "Accept" on our cookie consent banner. GA4 collects:

  • Pages you visit and how long you spend on them
  • Your approximate country (IP address is anonymised by GA4)
  • Device type and browser
  • How you found the site (search engine, direct, social media)

GA4 data retention is set to 14 months. You can withdraw consent at any time using the Cookie Settings link in the footer of every page. Withdrawing consent stops all analytics tracking immediately.

7. Affiliate Links and Tracking

Some pages on nextroutine.com contain affiliate links to products we recommend. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, the merchant may set a cookie on your device to track the referral, and we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

We do not control the cookies set by affiliate merchants. Each merchant has their own cookie and privacy policy. Affiliate relationships never influence our editorial recommendations — we only recommend products we would use ourselves.

All affiliate links are disclosed with a visible statement near the link.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format (CSV or JSON).
  • Restriction — ask us to stop processing your data while a complaint is resolved.
  • Object — object to processing based on our legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — unsubscribe from emails at any time; withdraw cookie consent via the footer link.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@nextroutine.com. We will respond within 30 days. There is no fee for exercising your rights unless requests are manifestly unfounded or excessive.

10. Data Retention

  • Email subscribers: data held while you remain subscribed. Fully deleted within 30 days of unsubscribing.
  • Pro subscribers: account data held for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days after cancellation.
  • Payment records: retained for 7 years as required by EU financial record-keeping regulations.
  • Analytics data: 14 months (GA4 configuration).
  • Cookie consent preference: 180 days in your browser's localStorage. After 180 days we ask again.

11. Data Security

The site is served over HTTPS/TLS via Vercel's global edge network. Email is transmitted via encrypted channels (TLS in transit). Stripe handles all payment data with PCI-DSS Level 1 certification. We do not store payment card data on any server we control. Access to our email provider account is protected by two-factor authentication.

12. Children's Data

NextRoutine is intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a minor has submitted data to us, contact privacy@nextroutine.com.

13. International Transfers

Your data may be processed outside the EU/EEA by our processors (Stripe, ConvertKit/Mailchimp, Google Analytics, Vercel). All transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent GDPR-approved mechanisms. We do not transfer data to any country or organisation without appropriate safeguards.

14. Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Malta Information and Data Protection Commissioner (IDPC) at idpc.org.mt, or with your local EU/EEA supervisory authority if you are based outside Malta.

15. Changes to This Policy

We update this policy when our practices change. Significant changes will be communicated by email to active subscribers at least 14 days before they take effect. The current version is always available at this URL.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

1. Who we are

NextRoutine is operated by NextRoutine Ltd, a company registered in Malta (EU). Our registered address and data controller contact is privacy@nextroutine.com.

We are the data controller for personal data collected through nextroutine.com and all NextRoutine communications.

2. What data we collect and why

Email address. Collected when you subscribe to the newsletter or purchase a Pro plan. Used to send you the free reset plans, weekly newsletter, and transactional emails related to your account. Legal basis: performance of a contract (newsletter subscription); legitimate interest (transactional account communications).

Quiz responses. The track-selector quiz stores your result in your browser's localStorage only. We do not transmit quiz responses to our servers unless you submit an email form immediately after the quiz, in which case your track result is stored alongside your email address to personalise your welcome sequence. Legal basis: legitimate interest (personalisation of content).

Payment data. Processed exclusively by Stripe (a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor). We receive confirmation of payment and your email address only. We never see or store your card number, CVC, or billing address. Legal basis: performance of a contract.

Analytics data. If you accept cookies, we collect anonymised usage data via Google Analytics 4 (page views, session duration, navigation paths, general geographic region). This data is anonymised at the IP level and does not allow us to identify you personally. Legal basis: consent (you must accept the cookie banner before analytics loads).

Affiliate link clicks. If you click an affiliate link, the receiving merchant may place a cookie on your device. We have no control over third-party cookies placed by affiliate partners. Refer to each merchant's privacy policy for details.

3. How we use your data

We use your email address to:

  • Send the free reset plans you requested
  • Send the weekly habit science newsletter (every Wednesday)
  • Send transactional emails related to your subscription (receipts, renewal notices, cancellation confirmations)
  • Personalise content based on your quiz track selection, if applicable

We do not sell your email address or personal data to any third party. We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to operate the service (email service provider, Stripe for payments, Google Analytics if you consent).

4. Email service provider

We use ConvertKit (or Mailchimp — see configuration at deploy time) as our email service provider. Your email address is stored on their servers in accordance with their own privacy policy. Both providers are GDPR-compliant and have EU data processing agreements in place.

Your email is transferred to our ESP servers in order to deliver the newsletter. This transfer is covered by appropriate safeguards under GDPR Article 46.

5. Cookies

Strictly necessary cookies (no consent required): We use a localStorage key to remember your cookie consent choice and another to store your quiz result. These are not tracking cookies and do not leave your device.

Analytics cookies (consent required): Google Analytics 4 cookies are only loaded after you accept the cookie consent banner. If you decline, no analytics cookies are placed. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser storage or by contacting us.

Third-party cookies: Clicking affiliate links may result in third-party cookies from affiliate partners. We do not control these and recommend checking each partner's cookie policy.

6. Your rights under GDPR

As a data subject in the EU/EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Ask us to limit how we process your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent (analytics), withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@nextroutine.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Malta Information and Data Protection Commissioner (IDPC) at idpc.org.mt or with your local supervisory authority.

7. Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you are subscribed to the newsletter. If you unsubscribe, your email is removed from our active list within 48 hours. Backups may retain the data for up to 30 additional days before being purged.

Payment records are retained for 7 years in accordance with EU financial recordkeeping obligations.

Analytics data is retained for 14 months (the default Google Analytics 4 retention period), after which it is automatically deleted.

8. Data security

All data transmission between your browser and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. We use industry-standard security practices for our hosting infrastructure (Vercel). Payment data is never stored on our servers — it is handled entirely by Stripe's PCI-DSS-compliant infrastructure.

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in a high risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours as required by GDPR Article 33.

9. Children's data

NextRoutine is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@nextroutine.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you by email (if you are a subscriber) or by placing a prominent notice on our website. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your GDPR rights, contact us at privacy@nextroutine.com. We aim to respond within 5 business days and will always respond within the legally required 30-day window.

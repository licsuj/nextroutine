Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 · NextRoutine Ltd, Malta

1. Who We Are

NextRoutine ("we," "us," "our") is operated by NextRoutine Ltd, registered in Malta. We provide daily routine plans and content for new and expectant fathers via nextroutine.com. Contact us at privacy@nextroutine.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what we need to deliver our service:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to the free newsletter or create a Pro account.
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
  • Analytics data — page views, session duration, and referral sources via Google Analytics 4 (only after you accept cookies).
  • Local storage — quiz results and email submission state stored in your browser only, never sent to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To send you the weekly NextRoutine newsletter and starter pack (lawful basis: consent).
  • To manage your Pro subscription and process payments (lawful basis: contract).
  • To understand how visitors use the site and improve content (lawful basis: legitimate interest — only after cookie consent).
  • We never sell your data. We never share your data with advertisers.

4. Email Service Provider

We use ConvertKit (or Mailchimp) to send email. Your email address is stored on their servers. Both are GDPR-compliant processors with Data Processing Agreements in place. You can unsubscribe from any email using the unsubscribe link at the bottom of every message.

5. Stripe — Payment Processing

Pro subscriptions are processed by Stripe, Inc. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified. Your payment card data is processed and stored by Stripe — never by NextRoutine. Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy.

6. Cookies and Analytics

We use two categories of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary: Cookie consent preference stored in localStorage. No tracking.
  • Analytics (optional): Google Analytics 4 — only loaded after you click "Accept" on our cookie banner. GA4 anonymises IP addresses. Data retention: 14 months. You can withdraw consent at any time via the Cookie Settings link in the footer.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a resident of the EU/EEA you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — unsubscribe from emails at any time; withdraw cookie consent via footer link.

To exercise any right, email privacy@nextroutine.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

  • Email subscribers: data held while you remain subscribed. Deleted within 30 days of unsubscribe.
  • Pro subscribers: account data held for the duration of subscription plus 30 days after cancellation.
  • Payment records: retained for 7 years as required by EU financial regulations.
  • Analytics data: 14 months (Google Analytics 4 default).

9. Data Security

The site is served over HTTPS/TLS via Vercel. Email is transmitted via encrypted channels. Stripe handles all payment data with PCI-DSS Level 1 certification. We do not store payment card data on our servers.

10. Children's Data

Our service is intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If we discover we have done so, we will delete it immediately. Contact privacy@nextroutine.com if you believe a minor has submitted data.

11. International Transfers

Data may be processed outside the EU/EEA by our processors (Stripe, ConvertKit/Mailchimp, Google Analytics). All processors operate under Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent GDPR transfer mechanisms.

12. Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Malta Information and Data Protection Commissioner (IDPC) at idpc.org.mt, or with your local supervisory authority if you are based outside Malta.

13. Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy as our practices change. Significant changes will be communicated via email to subscribers. The current version is always available at nextroutine.com/privacy.

14. Contact

For any privacy-related question: privacy@nextroutine.com

How NextRoutine works.

A short note on methodology, because most "expert" content sites don't tell you how their content gets made.

How protocols are built

I read the relevant academic literature — sleep research from peer-reviewed journals, behavioural science papers, the actual studies behind the popular advice. I synthesise it into protocols. I use AI as a research and drafting assistant, then I review, edit, and approve everything before it ships.

Where research is cited, the source is linked. Where I've personally tested something, I say so. Where two studies disagree, I say that too.

What this site is not

This is not medical advice. Sleep, anxiety, and mental health protocols here are research-based, but if you're experiencing severe sleep issues or symptoms of postpartum depression (yes, fathers get this too), please speak to a healthcare professional.

This is not parenting advice for the child. The protocols are for the father. Your child has their own needs and you should rely on their paediatrician for those.

## NOTES ON VOLUME ESTIMATES

Search volume estimates are based on typical ranges for these terms in English-speaking markets (US + UK + AU + EU English). Actual volumes may vary by 20-40% depending on the tool used (Ahrefs, SEMrush, Google Keyword Planner). Difficulty ratings are calibrated for a new domain with zero backlinks — "Low" means rankable within 3-6 months with quality content and basic on-page SEO; "Medium" means 6-12 months with backlink acquisition; "High" means 12+ months and requires significant domain authority.

Featured Snippet opportunities are flagged based on question-format queries where current SERP results show a Featured Snippet from a lower-authority domain, indicating Google is willing to surface quality answers from newer sites.
